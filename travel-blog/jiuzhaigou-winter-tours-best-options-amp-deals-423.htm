Jiuzhaigou Winter Tours: Best Options & Deals

Winter transforms Jiuzhaigou into a breathtaking wonderland, where frozen waterfalls, snow-dusted forests, and crystal-clear turquoise lakes create a scene straight out of a fairy tale. While summer crowds fade away, the colder months offer a serene and magical experience for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty. Here’s everything you need to know about planning the perfect Jiuzhaigou winter tour, including the best deals, activities, and insider tips.

Why Visit Jiuzhaigou in Winter?

Fewer Crowds, More Serenity

Jiuzhaigou is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning landscapes, but peak seasons bring heavy tourist traffic. Winter, however, sees significantly fewer visitors, allowing you to explore the park’s iconic sites—like Nuorilang Falls and Five-Color Pond—without the usual hustle.

Snow-Covered Scenery

The park’s already mesmerizing scenery becomes even more enchanting under a blanket of snow. Frozen waterfalls, icicle formations, and misty valleys create a dreamlike atmosphere perfect for photography and quiet reflection.

Lower Prices & Exclusive Deals

With fewer tourists, winter is the best time to snag discounts on flights, hotels, and tour packages. Many local operators offer special winter promotions, making it an affordable time to visit this otherwise premium destination.

Top Winter Activities in Jiuzhaigou

1. Exploring the Frozen Waterfalls

Jiuzhaigou’s waterfalls, like Pearl Shoal and Shuzheng Falls, freeze into spectacular ice sculptures during winter. The sight of cascading water turned into shimmering ice curtains is unforgettable.

2. Snowshoeing & Winter Hiking

While some trails may be closed due to snow, many remain accessible with proper gear. Snowshoeing through the park’s quieter paths offers an intimate way to experience its winter beauty.

3. Hot Spring Relaxation

After a day in the cold, unwind at nearby hot springs like Huanglong’s resorts. Soaking in warm mineral waters while surrounded by snowy peaks is pure bliss.

4. Cultural Encounters with Local Tibetans

Winter is a great time to engage with local Tibetan communities. Visit villages like Zharu Valley to experience traditional hospitality, warm butter tea, and seasonal festivals.

Best Jiuzhaigou Winter Tour Packages

Budget-Friendly Group Tours

Many agencies offer 3-5 day group tours covering Jiuzhaigou and nearby Huanglong National Park at reduced winter rates. These often include transportation, park tickets, and basic lodging.

Luxury Private Tours

For a more personalized experience, private tours with expert guides, premium hotels, and customized itineraries are available. Some even include helicopter rides for aerial views of the snow-covered valleys.

Photography-Focused Expeditions

Winter’s soft light and dramatic landscapes make Jiuzhaigou a paradise for photographers. Specialized tours provide access to prime shooting locations during golden hours.

Essential Winter Travel Tips

Dress for Extreme Cold

Temperatures can drop below -10°C (14°F), so pack thermal layers, waterproof boots, gloves, and a heavy jacket. Hand warmers are also a lifesaver.

Check Road Conditions

Snow and ice can affect road access to Jiuzhaigou. Confirm transportation options in advance, as flights to Jiuhuang Airport are more reliable than long bus rides in winter.

Book Accommodations Early

While winter is less crowded, the best hotels (like InterContinental Jiuzhai Paradise) still fill up quickly due to limited availability.

Respect Park Regulations

Some areas may be restricted for safety. Always follow park guidelines and stay on marked trails to avoid accidents.

Where to Stay: Winter Accommodation Picks

1. Jiuzhai Paradise International Resort

A top luxury option with heated rooms, spa services, and easy park access.

2. Sheraton Jiuzhaigou Resort

Offers cozy lodgings with mountain views and an on-site restaurant serving hotpot—perfect for cold days.

3. Local Guesthouses in Zhangzha Town

For budget travelers, family-run guesthouses provide warmth and authentic Tibetan hospitality.

How to Get the Best Winter Deals

Last-Minute Flight Discounts

Airlines often reduce prices for winter flights to Chengdu or Jiuhuang Airport. Use apps like Skyscanner to track deals.

Bundle Hotel + Tour Packages

Websites like Trip.com frequently offer winter discounts when booking lodging and tours together.

Travel During Weekdays

Mid-week visits (Tuesday-Thursday) are even quieter and sometimes cheaper than weekends.

Jiuzhaigou in winter is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. With the right planning, you can experience its ethereal beauty without the crowds—and at a fraction of the cost. Whether you’re chasing the perfect photo, a peaceful retreat, or cultural immersion, this season delivers it all.
